FULL TUNE UP - $35

A full tuneup includes the following services:

  • Truing wheels (aligning the wheels)
  • Tightening and adjusting brakes
  • Tuning and adjusting deraileurs
  • Lubricating gears and suspension
  • Inspecting bottom bracket and crank set
TIRE SERVICES - 5$ to 30$ (see below)

Save money by getting inner tubes ahead of time! (they can run as low as $5 dollars per tube on Amazon). If you don't have one, don't worry, I can bring you one. Get a $5 discount (applied to total) if you get full tuneup!

  • already have inner tube(s): one wheel - $10, both wheels - $15
  • no inner tube(s): one wheel - $20, both wheels - $30
OTHER SERVICES OR UPGRADES - depends on part, contact me for quote

Whether it's worn out brake pads or a busted wheel, or a fancier handle bar, I can replace/upgrade any part(s) you need for as low as 5 dollars (depending on the part), given that you provide the parts. Call/text me for details.
